04 · Methodology

My Evaluation Approach

01

First Read — Overall Impression

Read the full output once without marking. This surfaces immediate tone issues, logical flow problems, and whether something "sounds right" to a native Vietnamese reader — before any analytical layer.

02

Claim Isolation — Every Verifiable Statement

Isolate every factual claim: statistics, attributions, causal statements, superlatives. Tag each as verifiable / unverifiable / false. Cross-check against known facts; flag unverifiable claims for sourcing issues.

03

Tone Analysis — Register, Appropriateness, Cultural Fit

Evaluate on three axes: (1) register match — does formality fit the context? (2) cultural appropriateness — unintended connotations in Vietnamese? (3) editorial bias — opinions presented as facts?

04

Safety Screen — Flag High-Risk Content

Check for safety violations before issuing any rating: medical misinformation, dangerous instructions, harmful stereotypes, privacy violations. Safety flags always override quality scores.

05

Structured Documentation — Actionable Feedback

Every evaluation produces documented findings: issue type, specific location, severity, recommended action. Feedback is written so the next person in the pipeline can act without follow-up questions.
